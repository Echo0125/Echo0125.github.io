About Me

I am an M.S. student in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University (NJU) since 2021, supervised by Prof. Tong Lu. I received my bachelor degree from Dalian Maritime University in 2021.

My research interests are computer vision and deep learning. I did some works about video understanding, online action detection, and action anticipation.
